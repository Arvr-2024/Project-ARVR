Grenada

A Grenada is a small explosive device typically thrown by hand, launched from a grenade launcher, or projected from specialized weapons such as rifle-mounted grenade launchers. Grenades are designed to explode after a short delay upon impact or after being triggered by a fuse mechanism. They are used to inflict damage, incapacitate enemy personnel, destroy or damage enemy equipment or fortifications, and create confusion or disarray among enemy forces.
